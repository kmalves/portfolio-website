Me :)

Hi. I'm Kate. I started my career with a degree in Civil and Industrial Engineering and a job at a construction firm. It was intelligent and demanding, but lacked creativity and a great deal of my time was spent at messy and loud construction sites.
The first time I tried coding was for my hobby projects website. One article led to another and, three months later, instead of a simple one page site I originally had in mind I managed to put together a multi-page storefront. The process was amazingly rewarding! It was analytically challenging, technical, yet creative. Since then I've been coding for over two years with Udacity online programs and FreeCodeCamp, and loved every minute of it, as frustrating as it can be at times.
When I'm not working or studying I enjoy spending time with my family, outdoor activities, running, swimming, trying new recipes, making clothes and working on home improvement projects.
